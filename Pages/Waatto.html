Wappu posters 2020

30.4.2020

Blender, Photoshop

Two poster designs I made for 2020 Oulu's Teekkariwappu's main events: "Waaton aatto" and "Waatto". The Wappu theme that year was "vikings". Unfortunately all the event's were later cancelled due to covid. The posters were later renamed and used for another occasion.
