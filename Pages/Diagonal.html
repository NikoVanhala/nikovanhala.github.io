Diagonal


Revit, AutoCad, Blender, Photoshop

Diagonal consists of five longitudinal ”logs” spreading across the lot. There are lower spaces with green roofs for laboratories and -- between these office tubes. An eight-story Central Hub slices the building, offering passage and recreational spaces across the facility. The logs are facing south to maximize sunlight intake, and they leave room for active courtyards.

The whole building is comprised of massive wood pillar and beam system with horisontal steel reinforcement. Wooden surfaces have a large role in the interior. Not only is it a traditional and important Finnish building material, it also has many positive physical and mental effects on humans, and is seen to be an ideal surface to ensure a healthy work environment.

Honorable mention (4th and 5th) in competition.
