DIAGONAL

26.2.2021

Revit, AutoCad, Blender, Photohsop

My entry for Nokia's idea competition held for Oulu University architecture sudents in 2021. Diagonal consists of five longitudinal ”logs” spreading across the lot. There are lower spaces with green roofs for laboratories and -- between these office tubes. An eight-story Central Hub slices the building, offering passage and recreational spaces across the facility. The logs are facing south to maximize sunlight intake, and they leave room for active courtyards.

Honorable mention (4th/5th place)
