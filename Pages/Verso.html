Verso

3.12.2021

Blender, Revit, Photoshop

Verso was my entry for Science Center Heureka's student contest for an outdoor meeting and break place. Verso strives to bring together the humans' structural and the animal kingdom's haphazard and organic shelter. The wooden structures are laminated timber elements with a clear protective finish, and the canopy glazing is frosted plexiglass. The free form of the entirety is easily constructed and can be seamlessly expanded in the future. The weather sheltered area is sufficent for at least 30 personel.

First stage continuation place ( top10 )
