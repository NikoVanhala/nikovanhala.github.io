Stairs

2019 spring

Revit, Sketchup, Blender, Photoshop

As our second year architecture design course task we were tasked to design a stairway connecting two floors of our gallery space, which we had also designed in the previous semester. My stair design continued the organic theme of the building foorprint using the same materials used in the whole of the interior. The stairs are not a separate element, but more of an extention within the interior space, a seamless connection of two levels, emphasizing the flow of possible exhibition routes.
